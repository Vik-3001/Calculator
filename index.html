About

All-In-One Calculator is a simple, clean and easy to use calculator with a complete set of tools for day to day life, such as a unit converter, currency converter, percentage calculator and more.

From simple or complex calculations, to unit and currency conversions, percentages, proportions, areas, volumes, etc... it does it all. And it does it good!

Scientific Calculators

Performs complex mathematical calculations, scientific functions, and statistical operations.

Geometry Calculators

Calculate dimensions for common 2D shapes and 3D bodies using known variables. Solve right triangles with step-by-step solutions.
